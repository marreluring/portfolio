About me

My name is Marcus and I work as a UX-Designer at frog. My experiences include prototyping and wireframing, meeting users and customers, evaluating prototypes and many more of the methods related to user research. Industries where Marcus has experience from include automotive, prop tech (BIM) and e-commerce.

As a person I am team oriented and social, positive and keen to understand every technical aspect of a product or service. My strengths for completing this are that I am concerned with every team member should feel included in the work environment and my best tool for this is humor and being a good listener.

2018 - 2020

MSc. Interaction Design & Technologies

Interaction design is the practice of designing interactive digital products, environments, systems and services. It focuses on behaviour – how users act and how products respond to user behaviour. As such, it is a necessary discipline in any design project aiming to create something complex and computer-augmented that is to be used by humans: computer software, games, interactive products such as smart phones, “intelligent” homes, cars, clothes and so on.​

Given the increased focus on interaction design in general, and on social media, entertainment and new physical products in particular, the programme aims to provide interaction designers with the knowledge and skills needed to meet the ever-shifting demands of this emerging job market segment.
